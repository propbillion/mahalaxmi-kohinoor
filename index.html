    2, 3 & 4 BHK residences plus Smart Home configurations, with carpet areas from 830 sqft up to a 3051 sqft grand 4 BHK. Large-format vitrified tiles, 3,000mm floor-to-floor height, cross-ventilated and efficiently planned.

    80,000 sqft of sport and recreation operated by Ileseum Sports Management - grand gymnasium, indoor racquet courts, jacuzzi and leisure deck, steam and sauna, open-air courts, premium sports bar, designer cafe lounges and modern co-working spaces.

    Mahalaxmi Group, building since 2001 (steel) and in real estate since 2010, in partnership with Kohinoor Group, with Agarwal Sukhwani Associates. The sports ecosystem is professionally operated by Ileseum Sports Management.
